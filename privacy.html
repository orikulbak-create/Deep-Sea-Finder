Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Deep Sea Finder ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with our social media content published on TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, and YouTube.

1. No Personal Data Collection

Deep Sea Finder does not collect, store, process, or share any personal data from viewers, followers, or users. We do not operate a website with sign-up forms, do not run an e-commerce store, and do not offer any service that requires you to provide personal information.

2. TikTok API Usage

Deep Sea Finder uses the TikTok Content Posting API solely to publish original educational video content to our own TikTok account (@deepseafinder). We do not use the API to access, collect, or process data from any other TikTok users. No user data is stored or transmitted to any third party.

3. Social Media Platforms

When you interact with our content on TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, or YouTube (e.g. liking, commenting, or following), those interactions are governed by the privacy policies of those respective platforms. We encourage you to review their policies:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Instagram Privacy Policy
  • Facebook Privacy Policy
  • YouTube Privacy Policy

4. Cookies

Deep Sea Finder does not operate a website and therefore does not use cookies or any tracking technologies.

5. Children's Privacy

Our content is general educational content about ocean life suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our social media accounts at @deepseafinder.
